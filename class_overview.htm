Workshop Class Overview

Please note that this is an outline of the classes held on Monday nights at Wakefield High School during school years. For details about the classes, please click: Workshop .

CONTENTS:

1: A brief overview of the concepts of Traditional Chinese Medicine and its incorporation into our daily life to help us improve our health.

2: What's Chinese Clock? What's it related to our organs and body activities? (For details, please click: Chinese Clock.

3: Acupuncture meridians are like rivers or streets of a port or a city. They are energy channels.

4: When our body has cold symptoms, we digests food which nature belongs to heat to help our body fight cold. When we have fever, we eat food which has cooler nature to help us fight the heat?

5: What's Yin and What's Yang? Which parts of our body is Yin and which part is yang? How does that relate to the Chinese Clock?

6: Eugenia demonstrates how the O-Ring technique works to see whether the edible good or medicine is beneficial to your body or not as the Japanese Dr. Yoshiaki Omura had discovered.

7: Discuss various microsystems of ear, face, & foot reflexiology briefly. We also describe acupuncture.
