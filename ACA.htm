Acuremedy Community Acupuncture Center

Acuremedy Community Acupuncture (ACA) is a truly unique clinic offering patients affordable access to complimentary medicine. Based on the more traditional clinic design found in China, cost effective treatment rates maximize the patient's ability to receive benefit from treatment. Whether seeking pain relief, stress reduction or help with complex health issues ACA is a resource for better health you can afford.

The sliding rate scale of $15 - $40 per treatment and convenient location are designed to allow ACA patients easy access to the treatment they need. Click pricing to verify the suggested rate scale.

Our community acupuncture clinic is held only on Sundays and by appointments exclusively. Please call 617-678-1960 to arrange appointments. We are accepting cash or checks only at the present time.

The long history of acupuncture has shown patients receive the most benefit from frequent, simple treatments. Due to the rising cost of out of pocket health expenses many people have not considered complimentary medicine and feel frustrated by their lack of options in improving their health. ACA provides the community an affordable option for taking control of their overall wellness.

Other conditions that are commonly treated with Chinese medicine are: Allergies , addictions (including smoking), cardiovascular problems, weight loss, pediatric diseases, postpartum care, geriatric diseases, sexual dysfunction, low energy, migraines, fibroids, and urinary problems.

Acupuncture and herbs can also play a powerful role in helping people to quit smoking.
