a Male Patient with a Burning Penis

44 year old salesman Paul came to the clinic complaining about his burning penis . It was constant and persistent burning pain on the tip of his penis. Drinking coffee or alcohol aggravates it. It was worse since last ejaculation three days earlier. His doctor diagnosed it as Prostasis and prescribed VROXATHAL and ZINC. In the past, he was diagnosed with GERD (aka heart burn) for a long time before turning into hiatal hernia. It turned into burning genital a couple years ago. His doctor performed a certain medical procedure to clean it up. The procedure was not as effective as several meds his doctor prescribed. His doctor refused to perform the same procedure again when his condition got worse. His physician recommended acupuncture instead.

His face was red. His pulse was wiry. His tongue was red with thin white coat. His symptoms showed strong heat signs consistently through out.

After twice weekly acupuncture treatments and taking Chinese herbal formula of Long Dan Xie Gan Tang for two weeks, Paul was good to go and his pain was gone.
