Our Philosophy and Mission
Acuremedy - Acupuncture and Chinese Herbal Medicine Health Clinic

We bring Complementary Alternative Medical (CAM) care solutions to your health problems. Acuremedy is built on the principles of Oriental medicine (OM) applying a natural and gentle way to stimulate our ability to cope with a variety of physical, emotional, and psychological issues. In empowering our inner strength, we incorporate the OM methodologies and acupuncture techniques practiced by famous masters like Richard Tan and Kiiko Matsumoto in treating chronic conditions and restoring our body balance and health.

OM has proven to be effective in Asia for more than three thousand years.

Acuremedy brings this tradition to Danvers with personalized treatment and affordable services. In order to provide the most effective natural care, Acuremedy makes every effort in implementing its procedures and programs based on its four founding principles:

  • Focusing on our complementary alternative medical care of the root cause rather than simply symptoms, we encourage you to activate your innate healing energy to overcome chronic conditions and achieve sustainable health and wellness;

  • With our eyes on comprehensive care, we advocate benefits of improving your lifestyle by persistent holistic eating habits, exercises, and experiencing a remarkable increase in energy and vitality, while maintaining natural ways of healing through acupuncture and Chinese herbs.

  • With our location near major medical facilities (like Lahey Clinic & Beverly Hospital) and our holistic solutions that are complementary to Western medicine, you will benefit from the best of both worlds.
Acuremedy provides a safe, soothing and comfortable treatment atmosphere. We offer free consultations for your personalized treatment plan. Our services include Acupuncture and Herbal medicine. We also teach our patients new skills to take care of themselves in lectures, senior center classes and community adult education series. Since inception, we have effectively treated various chronic conditions. We strive to better integrate our therapies into conventional healthcare and further enhance their medical and cost effectiveness.
