High Blood Sugar and Diabetes

TCM practitioners treat and prescribe for diabetic patients based on differentiation of patrients' clinical symptoms. For example, if patients exhibit dry mouth & throat, anxious, insomnia, yellow urine or constipation, the case belongs to the deficient yin heat type. These are the symptoms often seen among early stage diabetic patients, or young people with physical dry heat type, or physically overweight or obese with high cholesterol issues or high blood pressure plus above symptoms. Treatments concentrate on clearing heat, nourishing yin, and moisturizing dryness. The main formula can rely on Zhu Ye Shi Gao Tang, Bai Hu Jia Ren Shen Tang, or Gan Lu Ying, etc.

Some patients have the chronic diabetes type. They may have taken western oral drugs to control their blood sugar level and stabilized its pattern. Patients' thirst may be mild or non-noticeable, but feel fatigue and lack of strength. This type belongs to the Qi/Yin Deficient type. The treatment strategy is to improve qi and nourish yin. The formula can use Shen Mai San, Yu Chuan Wan or Mai Meng Dong Tang, etc. These formula can apply to these cases whenever suitable.

For patients who have reached the lateral stage of diabetes with lots of other chronic systematic symptoms often accompanying other elderly issues and weakness; these include sore back and knees, frequent or/and voluminous urination, creamy greasy urine, may be diagnosed with kidney issues, or/and glaucomatic eyes with capillary blood issues. This type belongs to the kidney deficient with blood stagnation type. Formula can use Liu Wei Di Huang Wan, Ji Sheng Shen Qi Wan, etc. It's customary to include formula of invigorating blood and moving stagnation herbs to nourish kidney and move blood. If patients are easily tired without strength, easy to sweat, poor appetite, loose stools, it indicates a spleen and stomach qi deficient type. It is necessary to include Shen Ling Bai Zhu San to improve qi and strengthen spleen. Try to avoid using the nourish kidney or yin formula, otherwise it would double its effect on loose stools and make situation worse.

Foot Care

Please note to take care of your foot issues, if you or your loved ones have diabetic issues. While feet are farther away from the heart, it takes blood flow to go distance to reach; in addition, nerves are numb, sensitivity is low, it is easy to get infection, gangrene, or sometimes (in extreme cases) even requiring limb amputation. Therefore, patients should keep their feet clean, cut off their long nails, and try hard avoiding exposing their feet in too hot or too cold environment. Don't use electric heater. Don't soak their feet in hot water.

While picking out a new pair of shoes, please make sure your new shoes cover the front and the back of your feet. Both postures of lifting one foot on top of the other and crossing feet may make circulation difficult! Smoking may cause blood vessels to shrink, causing worse circulation condition. In case there is any injury or opening on your feet, it is necessary to go to physicians to treat. It's important not to ignore them.

Chicken and Limes Recipe:

There is a recipe circulating on internet for years. Some people found it helpful and others not. The idea is consistent with drinking Lemonade . Diabetic patients may want to give this recipe a try.

Ingredients:

  1. Buy a chicken from supermarket. Remove the neck, tip of wings, butt, and fat. Cut the chicken into 4 pieces.
  2. Boil the water. Put one half of the chicken in the boiling water for a couple of seconds, then drain the water.
  3. Wash 2 limes. Cut them into half. Remove the seeds.
  4. Put the chicken and limes in a double cooker and add enough water to cover the chicken and limes. Add some salt. Cook the chicken for 4 hours. Drain the chicken soup in a container.
  5. Repeat the step 4 and cook the chicken for 1 hour. Drain the chicken soup in a container.
  6. Store the chicken soup in the refrigerator.
  7. Remove the oil from the soup next morning. Warm and drink 1/3 of chicken soup with empty stomach in the morning for 3 days.

One chicken is good for 1 week. A course of treatment needs 4 chickens. After that, you can see your doctor to check the blood sugar level. If the blood sugar level drops to the normal range, cook 1 chicken a month for the maintenance.
