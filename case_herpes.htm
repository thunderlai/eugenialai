Mel is a 33 year old lady who has a herpes breakout a year and a half ago. When it flared up, her pains were irritating, burning, and interefering with her daily routines. She has been coming to our clinic for variety of reasons but started herbal treatment when she had a recent reoccuring flare-up with herpes a few months ago. Her body responded to our herbal treatment very well so she continued to get herbal formula for Herpes Prevention. She had success with acupuncture treatments, too. But she found herbal formula less invasive to her body.

Mel is currently on variety of medicines including: Prozac(20mg), Lamictal(100mg), and Trazadone(25mg). Mel has a good appetite, although gasy after meals with mild bloaty abdomen during last two weeks. She goes for bowl movements three to four times a day but she felt incomplete every time. She usually feels cold but has good energy. She felt irrited sometimes and sad and moody at other times.

The last time she had herpes flare-up, she felt tingling sensation and occasional itchy which can last few weeks in her vaginal region.

Her pulse is moderate and slight slippery. Her tongue color is Mild to moderate purple, slight Swollen and her tongue coat is greasy.

Dignosis/Assessment: LV fire and Kathy needs some Qi moving herbs

Prescription includes Huang Qin, Zhi Zi 5g, and Long Dan Cao to clear the heat and invigorates blood. She was instructed to drink it with hot water, three and half spoons each time, two times a day every day for two weeks.

This case was treated under the tutorledge of Dr. Tao Xie of Quincy, Mass in our herbal clinic during the summer and the fall of 2008.
