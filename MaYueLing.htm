Soak My Feet In Hot Water !!!

It also increases our body・s immune function. In this modern age, easy access to various bathing choices minimizes our inclination of feet-soak

as the singularly exclusive wash at the end of a day. Soaking in a hot bath or taking a hot steam can extend vessel walls, leading blood flows to limbs, and draining blood from essential organs and brains. Therefore, overly tired or weakened people after a long day・s work may find themselves dizzy, palpitation, or weak without strength after soaking in a hot bath or taking a hot steam. Soaking your feet needs high buckets. Through heat the meridian channels in the feet and fore-legs can relax, improving blood circulation, and increasing blood supply in the area of the heart and the brains. As ever-increasing flows of heat come running through, body may expire and sweat mildly. Channels are being soothed and moved via the movements, getting rid of cold qi and wastes, adjusting body temperature, and reducing deficient heat. Besides, as blood circulation improves, it can produce an obvious benefits to adjust blood pressure (either high or low ones). I recommend to friends that after a hard-working day they spend at least half an hour to relax and let body re-adjust. Don・t look down on this period of 30 minutes. Use wooden bucket. It・s best for keeping warmth. However, wooden buckets are heavy and expensive. It・s not convenient. Plastic bucket can be used as alternate. Most people choose the time right before bed as their soaking time. It is convenient, for one thing. It also helps sleep. If you have time, especially for elderly, it・s best to choose 5pm to 7pm. The endurance of heat varies from person to person. In the beginning, it is better to start with not so hot water. Then add hot water slowly. Keep the hot water coming to the bucket until the whole body warm with sweat.

Long term feet soakers don't have this issue. Only those people who just start soaking recently have the issue. This is due to the heavy deposit of cold dampness deep inside the body. Persistent soaking feet will rid of body・s cold dampness. Once cold dampness is expelled, it will be easy to sweat.

For them, not only soaking feet causes them to feel uncomfortable, but also taking bath would cause even more discomfort. Because the blood supply is low, more blood go to feet, blood supply of main organs shows shortage then. For these patients, I will tell them, the time of soaking feet should be less. It is important to eat right kinds of food, choosing foods that can nourish blood and kidneys. This is to fundamentally change the shortage of blood. Patients heeding my advices, in addition to augment nutrition, they also add suitable foot-soaks in their daily routines. Persisting this for 15 to 30 days, they would not appear to have tight chests or dizziness any more. 9. Is feet-soak suitable for children?

The goal of feet-soak is to improve blood circulation and replenish Yang Qi. Children are bodies of Pure Yang. There is no reason for them to have feet-soak. Washing by itself is enough. However, some kids have weaker constitutions. For those who have cold hands and cold feet, adults may give them feet-soaks in the winter. When they catch colds, feet-soaks in hot water ridding coldness is necessary.

10. Would feet-soak help improve dark circles around eyes?

11. Why am I sweating on my body but not on my head after soaking?

Sweating at upper body indicates deficient qi. Sweating at lower body indicates kidney coldness.
