Testimonial from a Multiple Scorliosis patient


"If you ever were inclined to try acupuncture but were unsure whom to go to, you have found the right person. My experience with Eugenia goes back to when she was still in school and working in a training clinic. Her caring attitude, eagerness and dedication to help not only impressed me then but continue to do so now. Her skills go beyond just acupuncture into the area of Chinese herbs. As a patient of hers for over a year, I would not hesitate to recommend Eugenia to anyone interested in these alternate therapies. "

-- per J.P.

JP is a 65 year old man who was diagnosed with multiple scorliosis (MS) more than fifteen years ago. His left leg has been limping and needed a prosthesis support wrapped around his left fore leg all the time. While wearing the prosthesis, he does not limp as much. But his condition is continuously getting worse.

Since he started acupuncture treatments on a weekly basis, the progression of MS has significantly slowed. He is also diligently working out in a gym four or five times a week persistently. He has also been taking Chinese herbs which helped resolve his other issues. Since his MS condition is relatively stable, he is now getting twice a month acupuncture treatment instead.
