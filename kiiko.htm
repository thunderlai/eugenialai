Kiiko Matsumoto (KM) style acupuncture was developed by Kiiko through her ability to integrate the works of Japanese Masters with her scholarly knowledge of the Chinese classics medical texts and her own findings into a clinically-effective style. She is internationally reknown for her scholarly studies of the classics and has published 5 books & many articles. Kiiko holds two very busy practices & teaches in US and abroad. She is also co-director of the Clinical Acupuncture Course for physicians at Harvard Medical School, & Beth Israel Hospital and teaches at acupuncture schools in NY and NJ.
