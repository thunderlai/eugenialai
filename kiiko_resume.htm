Kiiko Matsumoto, Lic. Ac.

Founder of Kiiko style acupuncture, Kiiko is internationally known for her scholarly work on Acupuncture and the interpretation of Chinese Classic texts. She has published several fundamental texts on Acupuncture in the U.S.

Kiiko regularly publishes articles in the Ido-No-Nippon acupuncture magazine in Japan and made two video series Kiiko Matsumoto International, Ltd. Kiiko is mostly known for her ability to integrate the work of very important Japanese Masters among them Master Nagano, Master Kawai and Dr. Manaka into a coherent clinically effective style. Kiiko is regularly teaching acupuncturists around the world and the United States.

Her vast clinical experience and powerful teaching style are her trademark.

Kiiko Matsumoto style acupuncture was developed by Kiiko through her ability to integrate the works of Japanese Masters with her scholarly knowledge of the Chinese classics medical texts and her own findings into a clinically-effective style. She is internationally reknown for her scholarly studies of the classics and has published 5 books & many articles. Kiiko holds two very busy practices & teaches in US and abroad. She is also co-director of the Clinical Acupuncture Course for physicians at Harvard Medical School, & Beth Israel Hospital and teaches at acupuncture schools in NY and NJ.

List of her publications includes:

  • Kiiko Matsumoto's Clinical Strategies in the Spirit of Master Nagano, Vol. 2, Kiiko Matsumoto & David Euler, 2008
  • Kiiko Matsumoto's Clinical Strategies in the Spirit of Master Nagano, Vol. 1, Kiiko Matsumoto & David Euler, 2005
  • Hara Diagnosis - Reflections On the Sea, Kiiko Matsumoto & Stephen Birch, 1988
  • Extraordinary Vassels, Kiiko Matsumoto & Stephen Birch, 1986
  • Five Elements and Ten Stems, Kiiko Matsumoto & Stephen Birch, 1983
