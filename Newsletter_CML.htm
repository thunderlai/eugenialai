Muscle

Pain can arise from anywhere in the musculo-tendinous unit, from the tendino-periosteal junction at one extreme of a muscle to the tendino-periosteal junction at the other. One of the most common types of muscle dysfunction that produce pain can be spotted by identifying trigger points.

Acupuncture is one of the best treatments for trigger points. Treatment is carried out acording to the simple rule of needling the most tender points. And this, frequently, is found by palpation.
