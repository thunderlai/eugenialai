Drink Lemonade For Health

COMMON SENSE- Drink more lemonade after work!

Wash the lemons, make juice, then freeze it into ice cubes. Seal them in a zipper bag and store them in the refrigerator for daily use. Making lemonade with the lemon juice ice-cubes is easy. We can all take advantage of lemon at its cheap prices and store them in icecubes. So we can always have lemonade to drink from.

Lemon is good for your heart and bones as well as blood circulation. Most working class has trouble replenishing their vitamin C intake.

Lacking vitamin C for a long period is detrimental to your health. Experts suggest lemonade drinks can be a remedy.

According to a Japanese professor at the Ministry of Ibaraki Christian University, the death rate of the cardiovascular and cerebrovascular diseases is far higher than that of cancer. Many office workers who often consume high calorie and high fat content of food, owing high blood pressure, and exercising infrequently are at high risk of chronic cardiovascular diseases. According to the professor's research, the first step in prevention of such diseases is to start with improved diet. He suggested that lemon being antioxidant with inhibition of free radicals is a good choice. Besides, people who have osteoporosis due to calcium deficiency can also drink lemon juice for improvement.

Lemon can enhance blood circulation and attract calcium. Moreover, the price of lemon is not that expensive. So, instead of buying expensive health medicine, it is wise to take advantage on lemon for health maintenance.
