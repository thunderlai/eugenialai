Chinese Medicine's View on Gall Bladder Stones

In western medicine when a gallbladder stone case presents itself, the physician's diagnosis generally can be summarized as follows:

  • Gallbladder stone has three symptoms: (1) jaundice; (2) gallbladder cramps; (3) Charcot fever.

  • The most useful tool in diagnosis is abdominal supersonograph. It is easy to operate. Its detection is high precision: some times as small as 2mm gallbladder stone can be found.

  • X-ray exam - I.V.C. or P.T.C.

  • Most likely candidate characteristics are Four F's: Female, Fatty, Forty (years old), and Fertile.

On the other hand, TCM diagnoses gallbladder stone based on clinical symptoms and differentiates into four types:

  1. Damp-Heat Type (mostly acute cholecystitis with gallbladder infection):

    • Main symptoms: it begins with urgent pain in the upper right abdomen. Nausea, vomit, poor appetite, thirsty prefer drinks. High fever, averse to colde, face & body yellow like orange. Upper right abdomen feels tight refuse being touched. ( pressure pain, jump at releasing pressure, enlarged & swollen liver & gallbladder, yellow or shortage of urine, hard and tough stools, gray or white stools. Tongue coat is yellow, greasy, or white & greasy. Pulse is wiry, intermittent, or flood & speedy.

    • Treatment: sooth liver, adjust qi, clear heat, benefit dampness, support the strategy by unblock interior and attack lower jiao.

    • Formula: Da Chai-Hu Tang (+/-) or Long Dan Xie Gan Tang plus Mu Xiang, Zhi Ke, Yu Jin, Da Huang, and Ying Chen.

  2. Qi Stagnant Pain Type (there is not obvious obstruction or infection in liver/gallbladder bile duct, stone, and chronic cholecystitis):

    • Main symptoms: temporary mild dull pain in the upper right abdomen, repeated belching, bitter taste, dry throat, nausea, vomit, poor appetite, or discomfort at below ribs after eating, mild yellow sclera, mild or moderate pressure pain at upper abdomen, clear or yellow urine, normal stools. Tongue coat is thin and white. Pulse is wiry or slippery.

    • Treatment: sooth liver, adjust qi.

    • Formula: Yue Jue Wan or Si NI San plus Mu Xiang, Da Huang, etc.

  3. Ulcerative Nodule (Gallbladder stone, cholangitism, gallbladder abscess or perforation, acute peritonitis, liver abscess, etc.):

    • Main symptoms: other than those damp-heat symptoms, there are alternating heat & cold, confused spirits, wild speech, persistent abdominal pain, tight muscles, refuse being touched or jump at releasing pressure, shock, etc.

    • Treatment: surgery as soon as possible.

  4. Zheng deficient Evil (Gallbladder stone with acute cholitis, liver dizzy)

    • Main symptoms: persistent dull pain, confused & unclear or dazed, strained facial color, low & faint voice, yellow & dark skin, sometimes turning blue or purple, even tendency to bleed; extended abdomen, mild pressure pain, enlarged liver, touch pain, short & yellow urine, dry hard stools, purple dark tongue, dry tongue coat like sand skin, wiry or deep fast pulse.

    • Treatment: aromatic open orifices, calm, clear heat & relieve toxins.

    • Formula: at liver stupor, it・s good to have An Gong Niu Huang Wan or Zi Xue Dan. If tongue is purple and dry, it・s appropriate to use Ching Ying Tang.

Better Health in Dealing with Gallbladder Stone

During treatment, it is important to take care and heed the following advices:

  1. During treatment, it is important to avoid depression, anger, mad, better adjust emotions and determination.

  2. Exercise three times a week. Each day a 30 minutes low level exercise may loose weight or maintain suitable weight. Reduce LDL and increase HDL.

  3. For cholitis patients, it・s important to avoid fried, dry-heat foods.

  4. Diet Principle: Pay attention to hygiene, eat at same time in same quantity. Small quantity frequently. Avoid over eat and eating too much.

  5. Meals content include low sugar, low fat, high fiber, and low cholesterol. Vitamins are good for prevention health.

    • low sugar, low cholesterol - too much sugar and fat can cause obese. Weightloss is the important route of prevention and healing gallbladder stone.

      For people who already have gallbladder stone, greasy food (no matter which fat or grease) usually stimulate gallbladder to shrink, cause it to feel discomfort. When limiting fat content in absorption, most patients・ pain symptoms is reduced or disappear. In limiting fat absorption, it is especially careful to control the fat saturation amount. Saturated fat may cause body to compose cholesterol making gallbladder stone worse. Therefore, it・s important to avoid using high content of saturated oil like coconut oil or palm butter as cooking oil.

    • High fiber content

      vegetables, fruits, whole grains, and whole beans etc contain high fiber, which absorbs acidic bile content and make it to go away with stools.

      While gallbladder stones have already caused discomfort, it is better to avoid food which can cause distention and bloat abdomen such as dried beans, onions, sweet potatoes, white radishes so to avoid making symptoms worse.

    • Low cholesterol

      Control intake of cholesterol come directly from food to less than 300 mg per day. Most vegetables and pure veggie products do not contain cholesterol.

    • Vitamins Research points out that vitamins A & E may prevent & treat gallbladder stones. Vitamin C can protect vitamin E from being broke down so it is appropriate to take it. Avoid stimulating and spicy add-ons like hot peppers, chili, wasabi, and mustard to avoid discomfort.
